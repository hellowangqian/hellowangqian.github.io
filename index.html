Qian Wang

I am a PhD student supervised by Dr. Ke Chen. My research interests are computer vision, zero-shot learning, machine learning, human action recognition. Before coming to Manchester, I got my Master degree in Biomedical Engineering (2013), and Bachelor degree in Electronic Engineering (2010), both from University of Science and Technology of China.
